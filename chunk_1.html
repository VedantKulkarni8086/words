English Vocabulary

Level 1 (Words 1 to 100)

English Words: 1 - 100

Note on Fluency
To bridge the gap between ``professional'' and ``native-like'' fluency, the secret isn't necessarily learning longer or more complicated words. Native speakers actually rely heavily on \textbf{phrasal verbs}, \textbf{idioms}, and \textbf{situational slang} in their daily conversations.
