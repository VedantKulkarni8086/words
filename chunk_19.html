73. Wealth, Poverty \& Social Class

Note
Words and idioms used to describe financial status, making money, or struggling with expenses.
1801. Affluent
Meaning: Having a great deal of money; wealthy
Hindi: अमीर / धनवान  |  Marathi: श्रीमंत / सधन
1802. Destitute
Meaning: Without the basic necessities of life
Hindi: बेसहारा / अत्यंत गरीब  |  Marathi: अत्यंत गरीब / दरिद्री
1803. Privileged
Meaning: Having special rights or advantages
Hindi: विशेषाधिकार प्राप्त  |  Marathi: विशेषाधिकार असलेला
1804. Underprivileged
Meaning: Not enjoying the same standard of living or rights
Hindi: वंचित  |  Marathi: वंचित
1805. Filthy rich
Meaning: Extremely wealthy
Hindi: बहुत अमीर  |  Marathi: अतिश्रीमंत (गब्बर)
1806. Penniless
Meaning: Having no money; very poor
Hindi: कंगाल  |  Marathi: कंगाल
1807. Make a killing
Meaning: Make a lot of money very quickly
Hindi: बहुत पैसा कमाना  |  Marathi: भरपूर पैसे कमवणे
1808. Scrape by
Meaning: Manage to live with very little money
Hindi: मुश्किल से गुज़ारा करना  |  Marathi: जेमतेम उदरनिर्वाह करणे
1809. Live paycheck to paycheck
Meaning: Spending all income on living expenses without saving
Hindi: महीने भर का ही गुज़ारा हो पाना  |  Marathi: हातातोंडाची गाठ असणे
1810. Breadline
Meaning: The poorest condition in which it is acceptable to live
Hindi: गरीबी रेखा  |  Marathi: दारिद्र्यरेषा
1811. Nouveau riche
Meaning: People who have recently acquired wealth
Hindi: नया अमीर  |  Marathi: नवश्रीमंत
1812. Old money
Meaning: Inherited wealth of established upper-class families
Hindi: पुश्तैनी अमीर  |  Marathi: पिढीजात श्रीमंत
1813. Rags to riches
Meaning: A situation in which someone goes from poverty to wealth
Hindi: गरीबी से अमीरी तक  |  Marathi: गरिबीतून श्रीमंतीकडे
1814. Upper crust
Meaning: The highest social class
Hindi: उच्च वर्ग  |  Marathi: उच्चभ्रू वर्ग
1815. Blue-blood
Meaning: Of noble or aristocratic descent
Hindi: रईस खानदान का  |  Marathi: खानदानी श्रीमंत
1816. Making ends meet
Meaning: Earn just enough money to live on
Hindi: गुज़ारा करना  |  Marathi: उदरनिर्वाह करणे
1817. Prosperous
Meaning: Successful in material terms; flourishing financially
Hindi: समृद्ध  |  Marathi: भरभराट झालेला
1818. Well-off
Meaning: Wealthy; in a favorable situation
Hindi: खाता-पीता / सधन  |  Marathi: सधन
1819. In dire straits
Meaning: In a very bad or difficult situation
Hindi: बुरे हालात में  |  Marathi: बिकट परिस्थितीत
1820. On the breadline
Meaning: Having the level of income of an extremely poor person
Hindi: अत्यंत गरीब  |  Marathi: गरिबीत
1821. Tighten one's belt
Meaning: Cut one's expenditure; live more frugally
Hindi: खर्च कम करना  |  Marathi: काटकसर करणे
1822. Roll in money
Meaning: Have a lot of money
Hindi: पैसों में खेलना  |  Marathi: पैशात लोळणे
1823. Live beyond one's means
Meaning: Spend more money than one can afford
Hindi: आमदनी से ज़्यादा खर्च करना  |  Marathi: ऐपतीपेक्षा जास्त खर्च करणे
1824. Keep the wolf from the door
Meaning: Have enough money to avert hunger or starvation
Hindi: भुखमरी से बचना  |  Marathi: उपासमार टाळणे
1825. Stingy
Meaning: Unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
Hindi: कंजूस  |  Marathi: कंजूस

74. Jobs, Labor \& Workplace Roles

Note
Terms related to employment types, climbing the corporate ladder, or losing a job.
1826. Blue-collar
Meaning: Relating to manual work or workers
Hindi: मज़दूर वर्ग  |  Marathi: कामगार वर्ग
1827. White-collar
Meaning: Relating to the work done or those who work in an office
Hindi: दफ़्तर में काम करने वाले  |  Marathi: पांढरपेशे
1828. Freelance
Meaning: Working for different companies at different times
Hindi: स्वतंत्र रूप से काम करना  |  Marathi: स्वतंत्रपणे काम करणे
1829. Gig economy
Meaning: A labor market characterized by freelance or short-term work
Hindi: अस्थायी नौकरियों का बाज़ार  |  Marathi: तात्पुरत्या नोकऱ्यांचे क्षेत्र
1830. Nine-to-five
Meaning: Standard office hours
Hindi: नियमित दफ़्तरी काम  |  Marathi: नियमित नोकरी (९ ते ५)
1831. Work the night shift
Meaning: Working during the night hours
Hindi: रात की शिफ्ट में काम करना  |  Marathi: रात्रीच्या पाळीत काम करणे
1832. Graveyard shift
Meaning: A work shift running through the late hours of the night
Hindi: आधी रात की शिफ्ट  |  Marathi: मध्यरात्रीची पाळी
1833. Entry-level
Meaning: At the lowest level in an employment hierarchy
Hindi: शुरुआती स्तर का  |  Marathi: सुरुवातीच्या पातळीवरील
1834. Executive
Meaning: A person with senior managerial responsibility
Hindi: कार्यकारी  |  Marathi: कार्यकारी अधिकारी
1835. Lay off
Meaning: Discharge a worker temporarily or permanently
Hindi: नौकरी से निकाल देना  |  Marathi: कामावरून काढून टाकणे
1836. Fired
Meaning: Dismissed from a job
Hindi: नौकरी से बर्खास्त  |  Marathi: नोकरीवरून काढलेले
1837. Resign
Meaning: Voluntarily leave a job or other position
Hindi: इस्तीफा देना  |  Marathi: राजीनामा देणे
1838. Step down
Meaning: Withdraw or resign from an important position
Hindi: पद छोड़ना  |  Marathi: पदभार सोडणे
1839. Promote
Meaning: Advance someone to a higher position
Hindi: पदोन्नति करना  |  Marathi: बढती देणे
1840. Demote
Meaning: Give someone a lower rank or less senior position
Hindi: पदावनत करना  |  Marathi: पदावनती करणे
1841. Onboarding
Meaning: The process of integrating a new employee
Hindi: नई नौकरी में ढलने की प्रक्रिया  |  Marathi: नवीन कामाची माहिती देणे
1842. Probation
Meaning: A period of supervision over a new employee
Hindi: परिवीक्षा अवधि  |  Marathi: परिवीक्षा काळ (प्रोबेशन)
1843. Severance pay
Meaning: An amount paid to an employee upon dismissal
Hindi: नौकरी छूटने पर मिलने वाला पैसा  |  Marathi: नुकसानभरपाईची रक्कम
1844. Pension
Meaning: A regular payment made during a person's retirement
Hindi: पेंशन  |  Marathi: निवृत्तीवेतन
1845. Perk
Meaning: A benefit to which one is entitled because of one's job
Hindi: अतिरिक्त सुविधा  |  Marathi: अतिरिक्त फायदा
1846. Golden handshake
Meaning: A payment given to someone who is made redundant or retires
Hindi: विदाई के वक्त बड़ी रकम  |  Marathi: निवृत्तीच्या वेळेस मिळणारी मोठी रक्कम
1847. Workaholic
Meaning: A person who compulsively works hard and long hours
Hindi: काम में डूबा रहने वाला  |  Marathi: कामाच्या आहारी गेलेला
1848. Slacker
Meaning: A person who avoids work or effort
Hindi: कामचोर  |  Marathi: कामचुकार
1849. Headhunter
Meaning: A recruiter of important personnel for companies
Hindi: अच्छे कर्मचारियों को ढूँढने वाला  |  Marathi: योग्य उमेदवारांचा शोध घेणारा
1850. Apprentice
Meaning: A person learning a trade from a skilled employer
Hindi: प्रशिक्षु  |  Marathi: शिकाऊ उमेदवार
