57. Philosophy, Logic \& Critical Thinking

Note
Advanced vocabulary to help you sound incredibly articulate when debating, analyzing situations, or solving problems.
1401. Paradox
Meaning: A situation containing contradictory elements
Hindi: विरोधाभास  |  Marathi: विरोधाभास
1402. Dilemma
Meaning: A difficult choice between two options
Hindi: दुविधा  |  Marathi: द्विधा मनःस्थिती
1403. Hypothesis
Meaning: A proposed explanation based on limited evidence
Hindi: परिकल्पना  |  Marathi: गृहीतक
1404. Fallacy
Meaning: A mistaken belief or error in logic
Hindi: भ्रांति / गलतफहमी  |  Marathi: तर्कदोष / गैरसमज
1405. Pragmatic
Meaning: Dealing with things sensibly and realistically
Hindi: व्यावहारिक  |  Marathi: व्यावहारिक
1406. Dogmatic
Meaning: Forcing one's beliefs as undeniably true
Hindi: कट्टर  |  Marathi: दुराग्रही
1407. Existential
Meaning: Relating to existence or the meaning of life
Hindi: अस्तित्व संबंधी  |  Marathi: अस्तित्वाशी संबंधित
1408. Ephemeral
Meaning: Lasting for a very short time
Hindi: अल्पकालिक  |  Marathi: क्षणभंगुर
1409. Status quo
Meaning: The existing state of affairs
Hindi: यथास्थिति  |  Marathi: जैसे थे परिस्थिती

Meaning: Mental conflict when beliefs contradict actions
Hindi: संज्ञानात्मक असंगति  |  Marathi: वैचारिक विसंगती

Meaning: Environment where you only hear opinions that match yours
Hindi: एक ही विचारधारा वाले लोगों का समूह  |  Marathi: समान विचार असणारा समूह
1412. Straw man
Meaning: A weak argument set up to be easily defeated
Hindi: कमजोर तर्क  |  Marathi: कमकुवत युक्तिवाद
1413. Slippery slope
Meaning: A small step that leads to a terrible chain of events
Hindi: खतरनाक ढलान (गलत शुरुआत)  |  Marathi: धोकादायक सुरुवात
1414. Burden of proof
Meaning: The obligation to prove an assertion
Hindi: साबित करने की ज़िम्मेदारी  |  Marathi: सिद्ध करण्याची जबाबदारी
1415. Empirical
Meaning: Based on observation or experience
Hindi: अनुभवजन्य  |  Marathi: अनुभवजन्य
1416. Nuance
Meaning: A subtle difference in meaning or sound
Hindi: सूक्ष्म अंतर  |  Marathi: सूक्ष्म फरक
1417. Dichotomy
Meaning: A division or contrast between two things
Hindi: द्विभाजन  |  Marathi: द्वैत / दोन भाग
1418. Rationalize
Meaning: Attempt to justify behavior logically
Hindi: उचित ठहराना  |  Marathi: समर्थन करणे
1419. Arbitrary
Meaning: Based on random choice, not logic
Hindi: मनमाना  |  Marathi: अनियंत्रित / मनमानी
1420. Heuristic
Meaning: Enabling someone to discover/learn for themselves
Hindi: स्वानुभविक (खुद खोज कर सीखना)  |  Marathi: स्वतः शोधून शिकण्याची पद्धत
1421. Tabula rasa
Meaning: A clean slate; a mind not yet affected by experience
Hindi: कोरी स्लेट (साफ़ मन)  |  Marathi: कोरी पाटी
1422. Ubiquitous
Meaning: Present everywhere
Hindi: देशव्यापी / हर जगह मौजूद  |  Marathi: सर्वव्यापी
1423. Vicarious
Meaning: Experienced in the imagination through others
Hindi: दूसरों के अनुभव से महसूस करना  |  Marathi: दुसऱ्याच्या अनुभवातून मिळणारा आनंद
1424. Zeitgeist
Meaning: The defining spirit or mood of a particular era
Hindi: युग की भावना  |  Marathi: काळाचा आत्मा (युगप्रवृत्ती)
1425. Paradigm
Meaning: A typical example or pattern of something
Hindi: प्रतिमान / मॉडल  |  Marathi: आदर्श / नमुना

1441. Celestial

1442. Telescope
