English Vocabulary

Level 16 (Words 1501 to 1600)

61. Conflict Resolution \& Harmony

Note
The exact phrases mature adults use to solve problems, stop fights, and create peace in professional or personal life.
1501. Mediate
Meaning: Intervene in a dispute to bring about an agreement
Hindi: मध्यस्थी करना  |  Marathi: मध्यस्थी करणे
1502. Compromise
Meaning: An agreement reached by each side making concessions
Hindi: समझौता  |  Marathi: तडजोड
1503. Reconcile
Meaning: Restore friendly relations between
Hindi: सुलह करना  |  Marathi: समेट घडवून आणणे
1504. Concession
Meaning: A thing that is granted in response to demands
Hindi: छूट या रियायत  |  Marathi: सवलत
1505. Truce
Meaning: An agreement to stop fighting for a certain time
Hindi: युद्धविराम  |  Marathi: युद्धबंदी / शांतता
1506. Peace treaty
Meaning: An agreement between nations to end a war
Hindi: शांति समझौता  |  Marathi: शांतता करार
1507. Tact
Meaning: Sensitivity in dealing with others or difficult issues
Hindi: व्यवहार कुशलता  |  Marathi: व्यवहारचातुर्य
1508. De-escalate
Meaning: Reduce the intensity of a conflict
Hindi: तनाव कम करना  |  Marathi: तणाव कमी करणे
1509. Diffuse
Meaning: Make a situation less tense or dangerous
Hindi: शांत करना (गुस्सा या तनाव)  |  Marathi: शांत करणे
1510. Apology
Meaning: A regretful acknowledgment of an offense
Hindi: माफ़ी  |  Marathi: माफी
1511. Amends
Meaning: Reparation or compensation for a wrong
Hindi: हरजाना या सुधार  |  Marathi: नुकसानभरपाई / सुधारणा
1512. Forgiveness
Meaning: The action or process of forgiving
Hindi: क्षमा  |  Marathi: क्षमा
1513. Grievance
Meaning: A real or imagined wrong causing complaint
Hindi: शिकायत  |  Marathi: तक्रार
1514. Arbitration
Meaning: The use of an independent person to settle a dispute
Hindi: पंच फैसला  |  Marathi: लवाद
1515. Mutual agreement
Meaning: A shared understanding or contract
Hindi: आपसी सहमति  |  Marathi: परस्पर संमती
1516. Middle ground
Meaning: An area of compromise
Hindi: बीच का रास्ता  |  Marathi: मधला मार्ग
1517. Give and take
Meaning: Mutual concessions and compromises
Hindi: लेन-देन (समझौता)  |  Marathi: देवाणघेवाण
1518. Olive branch
Meaning: A symbol of peace
Hindi: शांति का प्रस्ताव  |  Marathi: शांततेचा प्रस्ताव
1519. Water under the bridge
Meaning: Past events that are no longer considered important
Hindi: जो बीत गई सो बात गई  |  Marathi: घडून गेलेली गोष्ट विसरून जाणे
1520. Agree to disagree
Meaning: Resolve a conflict by accepting different opinions
Hindi: अपनी बात पर कायम रहते हुए बहस खत्म करना  |  Marathi: मतभेद मान्य करून वाद थांबवणे
1521. Find a workaround
Meaning: Discover an alternative solution to a problem
Hindi: कोई वैकल्पिक हल निकालना  |  Marathi: पर्यायी मार्ग शोधणे
1522. Smooth things over
Meaning: Make a disagreement less serious
Hindi: मामला शांत करना  |  Marathi: प्रकरण शांत करणे
1523. Mend fences
Meaning: Improve bad relations
Hindi: रिश्ते सुधारना  |  Marathi: बिघडलेले संबंध सुधारणे
1524. Extend an olive branch
Meaning: Make an offer of peace
Hindi: सुलह की पहल करना  |  Marathi: समेटासाठी पुढाकार घेणे
1525. Let bygones be bygones
Meaning: Forget past offenses or causes of conflict
Hindi: पुरानी बातों को भूल जाना  |  Marathi: जुने विसरून जाणे

Vocabulary to describe things that are hidden, confidential, or mysterious.

1528. Cryptic
Meaning: Having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure
Hindi: रहस्यमयी  |  Marathi: गूढ

1532. Classified

1533. Confidential
Meaning: Intended to be kept secret

1534. Top secret

1535. Leak

1537. Under wraps
Meaning: Kept secret

Meaning: In secret; out of public view

1539. Keep a lid on it
Meaning: Keep something secret
Hindi: राज़ को राज़ ही रखना  |  Marathi: गुपित लपवून ठेवणे
1540. Sweep under the rug
Meaning: Conceal something embarrassing
Hindi: गलती छिपाना  |  Marathi: चूक लपवणे
1541. Smoke and mirrors
Meaning: The obscuring of the truth using deception
Hindi: धोखा / भ्रम  |  Marathi: दिशाभूल / भ्रम
1542. Conspiracy
Meaning: A secret plan by a group to do something unlawful
Hindi: साज़िश  |  Marathi: कट
1543. Cover-up
Meaning: An attempt to prevent people from discovering the truth
Hindi: पर्दापोशी  |  Marathi: लपवाछपवी
1544. Hush-hush
Meaning: Highly secret or confidential
Hindi: बहुत गुप्त बात  |  Marathi: अत्यंत गुप्त ठेवलेली गोष्ट
1545. Sneak peek
Meaning: An opportunity to see something before it is officially available
Hindi: छोटी सी झलक  |  Marathi: छोटीशी झलक
1546. Tip-off
Meaning: A piece of information given secretly
Hindi: गुप्त सूचना  |  Marathi: गुप्त माहिती
1547. Red herring
Meaning: A clue intended to be misleading
Hindi: ध्यान भटकाने वाली बात  |  Marathi: लक्ष विचलित करणारी गोष्ट
1548. Ulterior motive
Meaning: A hidden reason for doing something
Hindi: छिपा हुआ इरादा  |  Marathi: लपलेला हेतू
1549. Hidden agenda
Meaning: A secret motive for doing something
Hindi: गुप्त योजना  |  Marathi: छुपा अजेंडा
1550. Unravel
Meaning: Investigate and solve or explain (something complicated)
Hindi: सुलझाना (रहस्य)  |  Marathi: रहस्य उलगडणे
